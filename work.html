For the subject spatial, we were allowed to make a hypothetical design for an (art) intervention near the bridge over the A2 to Maastricht. In the Netherlands, there are many places where art is put next to a motorway or on a roundabout.

×

For the course on colour studies, we were given all kinds of assignments related to colour. At the end of the course, we had to make a 'colour diary'. I chose to use the monochrome cooking assignment for my colouring diary. I made a three-course menu in the colour white. I designed the colouring diary in a leporello booklet that you can also fold out: on one side you see photos of the ingredients and on the other side the dish

For the performance subject, I really wanted to make an installative performance. But when is an installation performative? usually something is a performance when the maker takes the body as a medium or object or the image changes over a period of time. But how do you make sure it doesn't become a children's museum attraction?
